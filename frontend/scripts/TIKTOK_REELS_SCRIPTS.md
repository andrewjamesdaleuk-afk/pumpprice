# High-Impact Short-Form Video Scripts (TikTok / Instagram Reels)

These are 15-to-30 second video scripts designed to go viral without needing a massive production budget or showing your face. 

**Format:** 9:16 Vertical (Screen recording of your phone/laptop)
**Audio:** Trending lo-fi or aggressive electronic beat. Use TikTok's built-in Text-To-Speech voice (e.g., "Siri" or "Jessie").

---

### Script 1: The "M6 Rip-Off" Expose (High Anger / Virality)
**[0:00 - 0:05] Hook (Aggressive & Relatable)**
*   **Visual:** Open Google Maps. Search a route from "London to Manchester". Zoom in on a random Motorway Service Station (e.g., Watford Gap). Point a big red arrow at the 168p/litre fuel price (if you have a photo, pop it up briefly).
*   **TTS Voice:** "Stop getting legally robbed on the M1 motorway."

**[0:05 - 0:15] The Solution (The App)**
*   **Visual:** Instantly cut to a screen recording of `pumpprice.live`. Type in the exact same London to Manchester route. Set the slider to "0.5 miles".
*   **TTS Voice:** "The government forced supermarkets to publish their live fuel prices, so I built a free tool that maps them on your route."

**[0:15 - 0:20] The Payoff (The Saving)**
*   **Visual:** The map/list populates. Zoom in on a Tesco Extra or Asda that is 2 minutes off the junction, priced at 139p/litre.
*   **TTS Voice:** "This Asda is 2 minutes off Junction 14 and saves you £15 a tank. Link in bio. Never guess again."

### Script 2: The "Postcode Lottery" Shock (Curiosity / Local Bait)
**[0:00 - 0:05] Hook**
*   **Visual:** Split screen. Top half shows a fancy commuter town Asda. Bottom half shows an inner-city Asda just 5 miles away. 
*   **TTS Voice:** "Did you know Asda charges 10p more for petrol just because you live 5 miles away?"

**[0:05 - 0:15] The Tool**
*   **Visual:** Screen record scrolling through `pumpprice.live` on mobile. 
*   **TTS Voice:** "Supermarket algorithms change prices based on your postcode. So I built a free live tracker that hooks into their open data feeds."

**[0:15 - 0:20] The Call to Action**
*   **Visual:** Type in a local commute (e.g., Leeds to Sheffield). Click "Find Cheapest Stations".
*   **TTS Voice:** "Put your commute in to see exactly where you should stop tomorrow. Link in my bio."

---

### Script 3: Delivery Driver Profit Hack (Niche Targeting)
**[0:00 - 0:05] Hook**
*   **Visual:** POV shot of an UberEats or Deliveroo bag on a car seat, or a dashboard view at a petrol pump.
*   **TTS Voice:** "If you do UberEats or Deliveroo, you are literally burning your profits at the pump."

**[0:05 - 0:15] The Solution**
*   **Visual:** Open `pumpprice.live`. Type in a random start/end postcode representing a long delivery drop.
*   **TTS Voice:** "I built a free tool that uses the government data feeds. If you have a long drop to a new zone, put your route in."

**[0:15 - 0:20] The Benefit**
*   **Visual:** The list of cheap stations appears. 
*   **TTS Voice:** "It shows the absolute cheapest station on your exact path so you never overpay again. Link in bio."
